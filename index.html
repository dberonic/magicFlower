About us


Flower magic is an online flower shop specialized in costume-made bouquets and their design.

How many times have you wanted to surprise a loved one but had no time to get them something or had no idea what to get them at all? We if your loved one loves flowers (which is quite likely) we have a solution for you. Why not send them a custom-made bouquet of roses which will brighten up their day? In our costume made bouquets you can chose paper color, ribbon and roses which it will contain.

If you are not sure if the colors you have chosen would match or not, don’t worry about it. Or selection process is made so that your options change based on your previous choices which results with well-matched beautiful bouquet each time.
